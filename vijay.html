vijay

Vijay was born as Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar on 22 June 1974 in Madras (now Chennai), Tamil Nadu.[15] His father S. A. Chandrasekhar is a Tamil film director[16] and his mother Shoba Chandrasekhar is a playback singer and carnatic vocalist.[17] His father is of Christian Vellalar descent and his mother is Hindu.[18][19] Vijay was baptised at the age of 12.[18] Vijay had a sister, Vidhya, who died when she was just two years old.[20] Initially attending Fathima Matriculation Higher Secondary School[21] at Kodambakkam, Vijay later joined the Balalok Matriculation Higher Secondary School[22] at Virugambakkam and went on to pursue a degree in Visual Communications from Loyola College. While studying, at the age of 18, he asked his father if "he would launch him"
